All About Alex JS Jang

Hello, My name is Alex Jang, a senior at Babson College, concentrating in Entrepreneurship. I’m planning to graduate this December. Although I’m an entrepreneurship concentrator, I have decided to study accounting outside of Babson College after taking a leave of absence.

I became really passionate about accounting during the summer of 2019 where I had the opportunity to work as a deals intern at PwC for transaction services. It was my first time working for an accounting firm, and I truly enjoyed working with other accounting professionals and industry experts. During the first two years in my college, as an entrepreneur, I wanted to explore diverse areas of interest from IT, hotel industry, and sports management. However, even after experiencing these industries throughout my internship, I still could not identify where my true passion lied, until I went to intern at an accounting firm. As an investor who invests in many companies, I had a chance to get familiar with 10k and 10q reports and it was always interesting to learn company financials and how they are performing in their business. I found out that it would be truly meaningful if I can be part of an accounting firm and contribute my talent in accounting to serve the other investors by producing accurate and high-quality accounting report. Then I had a clear goal of becoming an auditor, so I took accounting classes to sit for the Uniform CPA Exam, and I’m proud to say I passed the exam during the leave. Throughout the process, I was humbled, thrived off challenges and I was hungry to learn and to be pushed out of my comfort zone. There were some times I felt frustrated and discouraged because it was so demanding and difficult, yet I still didn’t stop and pushed me forward. It has been one of my best growth experiences and I believe it was the best gift I could have given myself. Apart from career, I am a very active person who love sports and traveling around the world.
